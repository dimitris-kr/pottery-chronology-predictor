AI Powered Web Application for the Chronology Prediction of Pottery Data

MSc Thesis Project

Research Context

The system has been developed by Dimitris Krikonis as part of the MSc thesis for the Data Science & Information Technologies program at the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications, National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, in collaboration with the American School of Classical Studies at Athens with data from the Ancient Agora of Athens.
