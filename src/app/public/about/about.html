Pottery Chronology Predictor

A decision-support tool for archaeologists to estimate the historical period or exact date range of pottery, based on textual descriptions and images.

By combining machine learning models with expert feedback, the platform provides fast, data-driven predictions while continuously improving through real archaeological input.
